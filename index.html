About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Hongyang Jia. My research focuses on computer architecture, including LLM acceleration and AI-driven design of architectures and systems. I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2023.

Research Interests

My research interests include architectures for accelerating large language models, with a particular focus on 3D-integrated heterogeneous memory systems, as well as AI for computer architecture and systems. I am currently exploring kernel agents for high-performance operator optimization.
